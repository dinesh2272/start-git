Angela's recipe for the Bwst Cinnamon Rolls

Incredients

Fo the dough:

  • 3 cup warm milk/
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread floar

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup buttter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with thw yeast,sugar eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add tothe mixture.
  3. Add the flour and mix antil compined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.leave in somewhere to rise for hours.
